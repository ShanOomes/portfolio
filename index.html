About me

As a current Data Science and AI student, I combine a solid foundation in technical knowledge with a passion for innovation in artificial intelligence. My academic journey, which includes a background in game development and professional experience in web development, has given me a diverse set of skills that allows me to tackle complex challenges across multiple domains. From data analysis and machine learning to the creation of intelligent systems, I, Shan Oomes, am deeply focused on applying AI techniques to solve real-world problems, while continually exploring new frontiers in automation, data-driven decision-making, and algorithmic efficiency.

My experience in game development has sharpened my problem-solving and creative thinking skills, while my professional work in web development has enhanced my understanding of how to build scalable, user-friendly solutions. This unique combination of fields enables me to bring a fresh perspective to AI, blending creativity, technical precision, and user-centered design in every project.

Driven by a constant desire for growth and new challenges, I thrive in dynamic environments where innovation is key. My goal is to stay at the forefront of AI advancements, always seeking opportunities to expand my knowledge and contribute to the transformative potential of artificial intelligence, while drawing on my diverse background to push the boundaries of what's possible.
