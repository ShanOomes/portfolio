## About

**Section label:** About

> I'm Shan Oomes, a fourth-year Data Science & AI student at Breda University of Applied Sciences. I specialise in applied machine learning — building systems that extract meaning from unstructured data and turn it into something useful.

> Over the past two years I've worked with Equans on intelligent document retrieval, with NPEC on computer vision for plant phenotyping, and with Banijay on NLP-driven content analytics. Each project taught me something different about the gap between a good model and a working product.

> Outside of university projects I'm interested in reinforcement learning, robotics, and the intersection of language models with structured data. I like problems where the answer isn't yet obvious.

> I'm available from June 2026 for a full-time position in the Netherlands. If you're building something with data and need someone who cares about both the engineering and the outcome, let's talk.
